BOLDBROWS BASIC TRAINING

— WE CREATE THE BEST BEAUTY ARTISTS

The BoldBrows training is a special microblading training of the PhiAcademy that enables all students to become a successful PMU artist – with or without previous knowledge in the beauty industry.

Our basic training lasts 2 days live and, in addition to the Premium Starter Kit with the highest quality products, includes a theory part, an intensive color theory, exercises on artificial skin (latex) and on the second workshop day the joint work on the live mode
